## Design Notes

- Editorial utility aesthetic with bold hierarchy and calm spacing.
- Use tonal surfaces instead of visible divider lines.
- Critical actions should stay within the thumb zone.
- Reserve red for urgent or life-critical actions only.
- Use soft gradients on primary CTAs and glass-like floating overlays on map surfaces.
